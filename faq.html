Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and events

Booking & Payments

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for most events, and 9-12 months for weddings during peak season. However, we can accommodate last-minute events depending on availability. For corporate events, 2-3 months' notice is typically sufficient.

  • Cancellations 30+ days before event: Full refund
  • Cancellations 15-29 days before event: 50% refund
  • Cancellations less than 15 days before event: No refund, but we can reschedule
  • Force majeure events: Full refund or rescheduling
Please see our full terms and conditions for complete details.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, bank transfers, and company checks. For large corporate events, we can also arrange payment plans. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted systems.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for events over $2,000. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due 30 days before the event. For weddings and large corporate events, we can arrange a customized payment schedule. Contact our team to discuss options.

Events

Absolutely! We regularly organize events across the country and internationally. While we're based in New York, we have partner networks and experience managing events in multiple locations. Additional travel fees may apply for destinations outside our primary service area, which we'll discuss during the planning process.

We specialize in a wide range of events including:
  • Weddings: Ceremonies, receptions, rehearsal dinners
  • Corporate Events: Conferences, product launches, team building
  • Private Parties: Birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations
  • Fundraisers: Galas, charity events, auctions
  • Conferences & Trade Shows: Professional gatherings and exhibitions
  • Concerts & Festivals: Music and cultural events

We have a curated network of trusted vendors across all categories (catering, photography, floral, entertainment, etc.). However, we're happy to work with your preferred vendors if you have specific preferences. All vendors must meet our quality and insurance standards to ensure a seamless experience.

We're committed to sustainable event practices including:
  • Digital invitations and communication to reduce paper waste
  • Working with eco-friendly venues and vendors
  • Implementing waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Using locally sourced food and materials when possible
  • Carbon offset options for travel-related emissions
  • Donation of leftover food to local charities
We can tailor sustainability practices to match your event's specific needs and values.

Technical
